Privacy Policy for AssetAura

Last Updated: 01/01/2025]

Thank you for choosing AssetAura. We are committed to protecting your privacy and want to be clear about our data practices.

1. No Data Collection

AssetAura does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data to external servers. All your financial information and app data are stored locally on your device and, if you choose to enable it, in your personal iCloud account.

2. Local Storage

  • All financial data is stored exclusively on your device
  • If enabled, data backup is handled through your personal iCloud account
  • You maintain complete control over your data at all times

3. iCloud Integration

  • iCloud backup is optional and controlled entirely by you
  • Data synced to iCloud is managed through Apple's secure infrastructure
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data

4. Third-Party Access

  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use any analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not collect crash reports or usage statistics

5. Your Control

  • You can delete all app data at any time through the app settings
  • You can manage iCloud backup settings within the app
  • You maintain full ownership of your data

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the policy with a new effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us here

Note: While we don't collect any data, we recommend users follow good security practices such as using device passcodes and keeping their iOS system updated.
